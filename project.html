Our Story

A Place Where
Passion Meets Craft

Café Noir was born from a single obsession — the perfect cup of coffee. Founded in 2008, we spent over a decade perfecting the art of slow brewing, honest cooking, and genuine hospitality.

Every bean we source, every dish we prepare, every candle we light — all in service of one promise: when you walk through our door, you enter a world entirely your own.
